About

Laura is sustainability consultant with five years experience coordinating programs and research related to water and environmental stewardship.

Scottish but born and raised in Hong Kong, Laura has lived and worked across Jordan, Egypt, China, United Kingdom, Uganda and Bangladesh. Recently relocated to Silicon Valley to work on an environmental technology startup. Interested in innovation in water conservation, renewable energy and carbon reduction and sequestration.

Experience

Laura's work has focused on the integrated and participatory processes and systems thinking fundamental to the sustainable development of water resources and services. Experienced in sustainability, corporate social responsibility, environmental policy and water resource management.

Laura holds degrees with top honours in Water Sciences and Governance, Watsan engineering, International Development and Political Science. Before consulting, Laura worked across differnet geographies on a Zero Carbon building in Hong Kong with Arup, a transboundary water resource management program with the Nile Project, a water and sanitation project in the Syrian refugee camps and host communities with Jordan's Ministry of Water, a renewable energy project in Bangladesh with the Grameen Foundation, a sustainable agriculture project with Stanford Woods Center for the Environment as well as researching and designing a $3M urban water governance program for Oxfam.
