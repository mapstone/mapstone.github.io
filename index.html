About

Laura is a water and environmental resources management specialist with five years experience coordinating international programs and policy research related to water security, climate change and sustainable agriculture. Scottish but born and raised in Hong Kong, Laura has lived and worked across Jordan, Egypt, China, United Kingdom, Uganda and Bangladesh. Currently based in Silicon Valley consulting on water and environmental resources management and technology projects - interested in innovation in water quality monitoring and conservation, particularly in agriculture.

Consulting

Water scarcity is listed by the World Economic Forum as the largest global risk in terms of potential impact over the next decade. Water shortages caused by climate change, pollution, and increased human and agricultural demand place increased stress on the world's freshwater environments. Consulting services are available for private and public sector clients seeking cost-effective, sustainable, policy-based solutions to water supply issues and water resources management conflict. Please contact directly with questions and queries.
